Our Programmes

PROJECT ||EI!NASI

Our course is focused purely on indigenous narratives, with more than 15 years of in-depth research, combined with old accounts, this projects is specifically designed for those who beloieve it in their hearts tha there has to be more to our story! For the advancement of indigenous knowledge systems, revival of our people's indigenous awareness and pride, learning, documentation, narrating, and mapping indigenous isiNtu-Khwe-!Kwi-Tuu languages, and expressly showcasing their necessity and relevance in the present as well as the future, this programme exists. Come unlearn, learn, and grow with us.

Fee: R300 per month Enroll now

!KORA KOBAB-ADA

A core linguistic initiative centered purely on Khwemana, i.e. !Korakobab, the holistic language, the structural revival of the !Kora language, as well as the story of the !Korana themselves. This programme provides uncharted insights into the orthographies, semantics, enunciation, reading and writing, comprehension of the !Korakobab language, as well as a much deeper study of the broader history of the !Korana people. Restore !Kora ways, advance !Kora heritage.

Fee: R400 pm / R2200 once off Enroll now

KWAXHOSA ZIBENZA ZIBUTYA

An advanced deep-dive into isiXhosa ulwimi lwenkobe, as was spoken by our forefathers. Explore the vastness of isiXhosa nqo! We offer a whole new understanding of isiXhosa, moving entirely from the concept of "memorisation", and into the approach of embodiment of the language, history and beautiful story of amaXhosa. Ntinga ntaka ndini!

Fee: R400 pm / R2200 once off Enroll now
